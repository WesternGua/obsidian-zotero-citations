# Zotero Citations v0.2.4

## In-text citation mode

- Added a third citation mode: **In-text** (with Footnote and Endnote unchanged).
- In-text citations are rendered as author-year text in the editor, while remaining plugin-managed.
- In-text metadata markers are hidden during normal reading/editing.

## Word export behavior

- In-text citations are now exported as plain paragraph text (not Word footnotes).
- Footnote and endnote modes keep their original note structure in `.docx` export.

## Docs

- Updated `README.md` and `README_zh.md` to document the new mode and export behavior.
